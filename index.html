Search Marketing: The Magical Treasure Hunt

Imagine search engines like Google, Yahoo!, and Bing as magical tools helping us find things on the internet. Search marketing is like a treasure hunt, making your website more visible when people search for something online.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO is like planting seeds to grow a beautiful garden. It involves creating quality content, being popular, using the right keywords, and having trustworthy links from other sites. The more boxes you fill with quality, trustworthiness, popularity, and authority, the higher your website ranks in search results.

Paid Search: The Magic Spell

Paid search is like a magic spell that makes your website appear at the top of search results. You pay a bit to be more visible, especially when people search for specific things. It's like knowing what people are looking for right at the moment they search!
